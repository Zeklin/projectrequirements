## Overview

The order management platform will be the go-to location for Datum clients to view and manage their present orders. This pulls from data from the deal room.

## Objective

The order management platform pulls from all relevant input windows to provide a comprehensive portal to view incoming and outgoing orders from the Datum client. This will allow them to more easily visualize and maintain their short term workflow and prioritization.

## Context

## Assumptions

Tabulated into a large ledger, each order line will consist of the following
- Inbound/Outbound tag
- Ticket number
- Date
- Order Status
- Link out to further details

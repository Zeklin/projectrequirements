## Overview
A company's assets are anything it owns or controls that has current or future economic value. This includes items that are currently valuable or could provide monetary benefit in the future.
These resources can be tangible items such as equipment and cash, or non-physical things such as goodwill, reputation and brand.

## Objective
The Assets module is implemented to track and manage logical and physical assets.
By adhering to functional and non-functional requirements, implementing robust security measures, and offering comprehensive documentation and support services, the system will empower users to efficiently track, manage, and leverage their assets to achieve their business goals
